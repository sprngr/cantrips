Executive Summary

  • Engineering quality for core meta-skill scripts is solid (tests green, deterministic helper scripts present).
  • SDLC control plane is incomplete: no CI pipeline, sparse evaluation coverage, and inconsistent plan contract hygiene.
  • Most risk is in verification and governance, not in raw implementation.
  • Immediate focus: enforce gates (CI + schema + baseline evals) before adding new skills.
